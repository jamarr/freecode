A timeline of Steve Jobs Achievements.

  • 1955 - Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco, CA.
  • 1972 - He breifly attended Reed College before dropping out.
  • 1974 - He traveled to India, where he studied Zen Buddhism.
  • 1976 - He Cofounded Apple.
  • 1977 - Apple released the Apple II.
  • 1984 - Jobs introduces the Macintosh.
  • 1985 - Jobs was fired from Apple.
  • 1997 - Jobs return to Apple
  • 1998 - Introduced the iMac.
  • 2001 - The first generation of iPods were released.
  • 2007 - Steve Jobs introduce the iPhone at WWDC.
  • 2010 - The first generation of the iPad was released.
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passed away at home.
